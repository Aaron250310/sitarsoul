About Us



What Are We?

SitarSoul is more than just a band; it's a musical journey that bridges cultures and traditions. Founded in early 2024, our band consists of talented musicians from diverse backgrounds, each bringing their unique influence to create a harmonious blend of East and West.

Our Story

From humble beginnings to performing on grand stages, SitarSoul's journey
has been one of passion, dedication, and a love for music.
Our fusion of Indian classical instruments with Western styles has
won the hearts of fans around the world.
